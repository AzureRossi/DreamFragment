Abstract

Despite recent advances in text-conditioned 3D generation, compositional multi-object scene generation remains challenging, particularly when modeling complex object interactions (e.g. A bird is perched on a tree branch). Existing methods typically struggle with realistic interactions due to overly coarse modeling of inter-object relations and spatial arrangements. We address these limitations by enhancing fine-grained instance relation modeling throughout the generation pipeline. Our approach features an instance-aware initialization strategy, which leverages the holistic scene generation capabilities of feed-forward reconstruction models, e.g. Large Multi-View Gaussian Model (LGM). Meanwhile, the strategy also maintains instance separability throughout the full scene generation process. We incorporate an instance-aware diffusion prior to effectively alleviate the limitations of Score Distillation Sampling-based (SDS-based) methods in modeling multi-object spatial relationships. Additionally, we integrate Neural Category Field into 3D Gaussian Splatting for fine-grained layout representation, reducing object-intersecting artifacts common in previous approaches. Extensive experiments validate the effectiveness of our method, which achieves state-of-the-art performance in both visual quality and quantitative metrics for generating realistic multi-object scenes with complex interactions.

Method

DreamFragment includes three modules: Instance-Aware initialization with pixel-to-feature Gaussian Segmentation, Layout Optimization with Category Field Guided Rendering & instance-aware diffusion prior, and Appearance Refinement with Boundary-Aware Pruning.
Using 3D Gaussian as the 3D representation, our method, DreamFragment, follows the typical initialization-then optimization framework for compositional multi-object generation. The overall pipeline of our approach is illustrated in Fig. 2. Given the input text description, our DreamFragment first initializes the 3D Gaussians of individual instances by a novel instance-aware initialization strategy. Based on the coarse spatial structure and position of each instance, DreamFragment proceeds through a two-stage optimization process, comprising layout optimization and appearance refinement. With the proposed fine-grained instance relation modeling throughout the generation process, DreamFragment achieves state-of-the-art performance in both visual quality and quantitative metrics.
